# Summary

A simple attachment to fix the Anet A8 or other printer to the basement. My threaded rods were a little bit bend so i made these straightforward attachment to mount the printer on a wooden plate. Between this plate and the tabletop I put four rubber bands, to protect the surface from scratches and decrease vibrations a little. I've used six of them, but four should be sufficient. The more, the better...

You should refrain from using countersunk screws to avoid splitting. A better option is to use cylinder head screws or washers.
